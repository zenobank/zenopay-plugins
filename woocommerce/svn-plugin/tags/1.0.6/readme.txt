== Description ==

Zeno lets you **accept crypto** payments in WooCommerce while **you get paid in fiat**. 


Stop losing 3% in fees per sale.

You pay a **flat $0.10 fee per order**.

[Try checkout demo](https://demo.zenobank.io/)

## What the data says

- 1 in 5 consumers choose crypto even when fiat is available
- Over 600 million people already use stablecoins to pay online
- 8% of online sales are already made using crypto



## Why install this plugin

• Customers can pay with crypto  
• You still receive money in your bank in fiat  
• Zero crypto knowledge required  
• No chargebacks  
• Instant payouts  
• Works alongside your current gateways
• Reach a global audience of customers who prefer paying with crypto

### Supported methods

• On chain payments  
• Binance Pay

== Installation ==

1. Install the plugin  
2. Create your account at Zeno Bank  
3. Copy your API key  
4. Paste it in WooCommerce settings  
5. Done. Your store now accepts crypto

Start saving money on every sale.
